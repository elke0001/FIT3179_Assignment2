Air Pollution caused up to 23% out of all deaths thoroughout the year 2000 to 2019.

GHG Emissions from Land Use and Forestry

Land Use Land-Use Change and Forestry (LULUCF) emissions contribute significantly to climate change.

The largest source of LULUCF emissions comes from deforestation, primarily in South-East Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

The primary greenhouse gases released through LULUCF activities include carbon dioxide (CO2), methane (CH4), and nitrous oxide (N2O).
